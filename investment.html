What is Exchange-Traded Funds Plan?

An ETF (Exchange-Traded Fund) is a type of investment fund that holds a collection of assets like stocks, bonds, or commodities, and is traded on stock exchanges, similar to individual stocks. It is a simple, low-cost way to invest in many different things at once, and you can trade it like a stock.

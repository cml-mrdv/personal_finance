Registered Retirement Savings Plan

An RRSP (Registered Retirement Savings Plan) is a type of investment account in Canada designed to help individuals save for retirement. It’s a great way to save for retirement, lower your taxes today, and let your savings grow without paying tax on the gains until later.

Stock

A stock (also called a share or equity) is a small ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, you’re buying a tiny piece of that company, which means you own a part of it. Example: If you buy 10 shares of Apple stock at $150 per share, you own a small piece of Apple. If the stock price rises to $200 per share, you could sell your shares for a profit. If Apple pays dividends, you may also get regular payments.
